Retell AI is the engine behind your cold calling system. Here's exactly what it is, how it works, and why it's going to change the way you work leads.

What Is Retell AI?

Retell AI is a platform that lets you build an AI-powered voice agent — essentially a phone caller that sounds like a real person, follows a custom script, and can hold a genuine two-way conversation with your leads.

Think of it this way: right now, working a lead list means you sitting down, dialing number after number, leaving voicemails, and hoping someone picks up. With Retell AI, that entire process happens automatically. The AI makes the calls, talks to the leads, handles common questions, and books appointments directly onto your calendar — all without you lifting a finger.

It was built specifically for businesses that need to reach a lot of people efficiently. For a life insurance agent like you, that means more conversations happening, more appointments getting booked, and more time for you to do what you do best — close.

How It Works — Step by Step
01

Your leads go into the system

Whether your leads come from your upline, a spreadsheet, or another source — they get loaded into Pipedrive and queued up for the AI to call. No manual dialing. No sorting through a notebook. The list is ready to go.

02

The AI makes the call

Retell AI dials each lead using a real phone number provisioned specifically for you. It introduces itself, follows your custom script, and responds naturally to whatever the lead says. It's not reading from a rigid menu — it's having a real conversation.

03

Interested leads get booked on the spot

When a lead expresses interest, the AI checks your live calendar availability and books the appointment right there during the call. The lead gets a confirmation. You get a notification. Done.

04

Every call is logged and recorded

After each call, Retell saves a full recording and transcript. You can review exactly what was said, how leads responded, and what the outcome was — giving you real insight into what's working and what can be improved.

05

You show up for the meeting

That's it. Your only job is to show up for the appointments that are already on your calendar and do what you're good at. The AI handled everything before that point.
