One-Time Setup Services
Powered by Retell AI
Your 24/7 AI Cold Caller
$400one-time setup

Retell AI is a voice-powered artificial intelligence platform that makes outbound calls on your behalf — using a custom script, handling conversations naturally, and booking appointments automatically. Think of it as a tireless assistant who works around the clock and never has a bad day.

  • Calls through your lead list automatically — no manual dialing required
  • Uses a custom script written specifically for life insurance conversations
  • When a lead says yes, it books them on your calendar on the spot
  • You only show up for the appointments — the AI handles all the legwork
  • Works while you're with clients, sleeping, or off the clock
Included with Retell AI Setup
Cal.com — Your Automated Appointment Book
Included Free

Cal.com is a free, modern scheduling tool that plugs directly into Retell AI. The moment a lead says yes to a meeting, it's booked on your calendar instantly — no back-and-forth, no phone tag, no missed opportunities.

  • Connects natively with Retell AI — purpose-built for this exact setup
  • You wake up with appointments already on your calendar
  • Sends automatic reminders to leads so they actually show up
  • 100% free — zero monthly cost to you
A note on Calendly: You may already be familiar with Calendly. While it works well for manual booking, it requires additional paid tools to connect with an AI caller like Retell. Cal.com does the same job — for free — and integrates directly. You won't notice a difference in day-to-day use.

Scope of Work
Week 1
Foundation & Account Setup
  • Create and configure your Pipedrive CRM account — custom pipeline stages, fields, and settings tailored to life insurance sales
  • Create and configure your Cal.com account — availability settings, appointment types, and calendar sync
  • Create your Retell AI account and begin platform orientation
  • Initial walkthrough session with Christelle — getting familiar with each tool, what it does, and how they connect
Week 2
AI Caller Build & Scripting
  • Write and refine a custom AI call script — tailored to life insurance outreach, FFL positioning, and Christelle's natural communication style
  • Build the AI voice agent inside Retell — configure voice, tone, pacing, and objection handling
  • Connect Retell AI to Cal.com — test the full flow from call to booked appointment
  • Internal testing round: run test calls, review transcripts, adjust script as needed
Week 3
Integration, Lead Flow & Testing
  • Connect Retell AI to Pipedrive — booked appointments and call outcomes log automatically
  • Set up phone number provisioning through Retell for outbound calling
  • Configure lead entry workflow in Pipedrive — clean, repeatable process for loading upline leads into the system
  • End-to-end system test: lead enters Pipedrive → AI dials → appointment books on Cal.com → records in CRM
  • Second walkthrough session with Christelle — live demo of the full system working together
Week 4
Launch, Training & Handoff
  • Final QA pass — review all integrations, test call quality, verify appointment booking and CRM logging
  • In-person training session: Christelle learns how to load leads, monitor the AI, review call logs, and manage her calendar
  • Deliver a simple one-page quick-reference guide — step-by-step cheat sheet for daily use
  • System goes live — AI begins working leads on Christelle's behalf
  • Monthly retainer begins — ongoing support, tutoring, and optimization
Lead Import & Data Migration: The process for importing existing leads from upline management into Pipedrive will be scoped and discussed separately based on the volume, format, and source of those leads. This will be planned collaboratively to ensure a clean, organized pipeline from day one.
